From a young age, I had a fascination with electronics and a natural inclination to tinker with them. This led me to upgrade the RAM on my first laptop at the age of 13, an endeavour that I initially believed had resulted in the device being irreparably damaged. However, to my delight, it actually worked, igniting a passion in me for electronics and setting me on a path toward a career in this field. When I was 15, I experienced the common frustration of an iPhone screen breaking, and after receiving exorbitant quotes from repair shops, I turned to self-reliance. Following a YouTube tutorial, I successfully repaired my phone with a new screen that I had ordered online for just $30. I realized that I could offer this same affordable service to others, and thus began my foray into electronics repair. Believing that more affordable repair options were necessary, I began learning the trade and honing my skills as a high school student. When I had repaired about 50 devices, I had gained enough confidence to start my own brand. I focused on building a positive reputation through social media and word-of-mouth referrals, utilizing reviews from satisfied customers to showcase my abilities and gain the trust of potential clients.
